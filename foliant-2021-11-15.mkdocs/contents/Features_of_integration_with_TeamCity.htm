Features of integration with TeamCity

Thanks to the Meta-Runner functionality offered by the TeamCity orchestration tool, it’s possible to reduce even more human participation in the integration (on-boarding) process and reduce the number of manual operations.

Meta-runner is a specially developed configuration file that allows you to automate the TeamCity configuration process considering the specific requirements of Maverix integration. Templates, automatic recognition of codebases and artifacts by URL, etc. are actively used during this process.
