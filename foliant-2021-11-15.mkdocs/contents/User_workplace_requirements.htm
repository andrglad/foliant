User workplace requirements

Hardware requirements

Windows, Linux, and MacOS computers can be used as a Maverix client computer.

Recommended Maverix client computer specifications:

  • Processor: 2 GHz or higher
  • RAM: at least 8 GB
  • Free disk space: 10 Gb (+ size of applied metadata).

Software requirements

Use one of the following browsers on the client computer:

  • Google Chrome version 78 and higher
  • Mozilla Firefox version 50 and higher
  • Microsoft Edge version 80 and higher
  • Safari version 10 and higher

Cookies must be allowed in the browser settings.
